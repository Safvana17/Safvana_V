Project - 1

CocoNutiX-cocunut leaf disease identification system.

The implemented COCONUT LEAF IDENTIFICATION SYSTEM, is a socially rel- evant system that helps the farmers to identify whether the coconut leaf is diseased or not. This android application is developed in such a way that it is more flexible and scalable. The system generate a report that shows the percentage of occurance of coconut leaf diseases such as leaf spot, leaf blight and leaf yellowing and healthy leaves. Hence the system is maintained with minimum effort of modification. The proposed system has been developed with much care that is free of error and it is efficient. CNN algorithm is selected with 82.03% of accuracy for training a model for disease identification and 97.22% of accuracy for training a model coconut leaf identificaion.
